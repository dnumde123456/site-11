Welcome to Site-11

Site-11, located deep underground and concealed within the vast network of SCP Foundation facilities, stands as one of the foremost hubs for the containment and study of some of the most perilous anomalies. Established in 19██, this facility has seen numerous incidents, each contributing to its extensive security measures and protocols.

Beyond its heavy security, Site-11 also acts as a research hub for studying anomalies, including those classified as Keter and Thaumiel. The site features advanced containment units and employs the latest technology in anomalous containment and security.

Recent upgrades to Site-11 include enhanced AI monitoring systems and bio-secure access points, ensuring a rigorous control over all personnel and entities within its confines.

Contact

Contact with Site-11 is restricted to authorized personnel. Unauthorized communication attempts are logged and reviewed.

Contact Director ███████ via the Site-11 Secure Line or through the SCP Foundation's encrypted communication network.
